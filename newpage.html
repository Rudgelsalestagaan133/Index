About us

I'm Shiela Loyola the owner of Gav's Homebaked Sweets. At first,I didn't expected that I some potential in baking,but I really liked to watch videos on facebook and youtube about baking.I'm curious and I want to learn how to bake so I did try to bake a chocolate chip cookies for my kids and in my first experience it's not really perfect and some of the cookies that I baked was burned into pieces, but still I did my best to make it perfect.Since in youtube they dont actually show all the ingredients and I was so curios that I actually tried to make my own cookies with some different ingredients and luckily it turns out good. I took an online class to enhance my talent and to learned more.After that I make a facebook page which is the Gav's Homebaked sweets so that I can start my small business.The store name Gav's is from the name of my two children which is Gavin and Gavriella.In my small business many peo- ple order from me when there is an occasion or just for thier cravings.I start my small business for my extra income since January 2021 and until now I have a lot of customer and still going strong .
